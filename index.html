Welcome!

My name is Adam Szava, I am a graduate student in applied mathematics currently studying at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU) under the supervision of Dr. Anthony Bonato. I did my undergrad at TMU in Computer Engineering. My research is aimed towards pursuit evasion games on graphs.

This website is meant to be a hub for the various projects I'm working on.
